2. Resolved a critical engineering question

The original proposal stated Cosmos uses T5-XXL cross-attention "like Flux". Verified against the diffusers source:

  • Cosmos-Predict2 (2B Text2Image and Video2World) uses T5-11B, max_seq_length 512, default 35 inference steps.
  • Cosmos Policy is fine-tuned from Video2World (NOT Text2Image). For a clean H2b/H2c paired comparison, both must use Video2World.
  • Cross-attention layers in CosmosTransformer3DModel are named attn2; we register a custom AttentionProcessor (since attention-map-diffusers does not support Cosmos).
